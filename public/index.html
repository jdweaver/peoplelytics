THE SCIENCE BEHIND THE PEOPLELYTICS RESEARCH PROJECT

The Peoplelytics research project was started with the goal of helping companies hire faster, smarter, and safer. It's based on the science of work and language.

Scientists who study the world of work have found that asking questions about people's past behavior is often predictive of their future behavior when questions are asked in relation to specific criterion like doing good work. Similarly, researchers have been able to link the words people use to things like personality, mood, depression, gender, and behavior. The Peoplelytics Research Project offers a way to better understand and predict behavior in the workplace by allowing us to analyze the words people use as indicators of people's behavioral patterns.
